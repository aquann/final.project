Theme Song Quiz Game

To win the quiz game the user must hear multiple theme songs from iconic movies/shows and pair them with their correct GIF. The user has the option of playing back the song as many times as she or he chooses. They also have the option of increasing/decreasing the volume if needed, and can also download the song if they like it enough. After answering all the question they simply need to click submit and wait for their results. To win you need to score 6 and above, 5 and below you lose. Good luck!
